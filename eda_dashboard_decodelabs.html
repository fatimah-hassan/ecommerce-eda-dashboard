Key findings & business insights

Skewed revenue distribution

Mean ($955) >> Median ($687) — high-value outlier orders are pulling the average up. Use median for typical order analysis.

High cancellation + return rate

~40% of orders are either cancelled or returned — a serious fulfilment/satisfaction problem worth investigating by product.

SAVE10 is most popular coupon

86% of orders use some coupon. SAVE10 leads, suggesting price-sensitive customers. Consider testing coupon-free strategies.

Revenue skewed right — outlier orders

Max total price is $3,456 vs min $11.39 — a 300x range. These outliers are signals worth investigating (bulk orders or VIP buyers).
